Convert CamelCase and other weird column name formats to pot_hole case.

Examples

>>> format_columns('HelloWorld')
'hello_world'
>>> format_columns('Hello_World')
'hello_world'
>>> format_columns('hg19_pos(1-based)')
'hg19_pos_1based'
>>> format_columns(['Hello (World?)', 'allGood?'])
['hello_world', 'all_good']

kmtools.df_tools._pandas.remove_duplicate_columns(df, keep_first=True, add_suffix=False)[source]¶

Remove duplicate columns.

Examples

>>> import pandas as pd
>>> df = pd.DataFrame([[1,2,3], [4,5,6], [7,8,9]], columns=['a', 'b', 'a'])

>>> remove_duplicate_columns(df)
   a  b
0  1  2
1  4  5
2  7  8
>>> remove_duplicate_columns(df, keep_first=False)
   b  a
0  2  3
1  5  6
2  8  9
>>> remove_duplicate_columns(df, add_suffix=True)
   a  b a_2
0  1  2   3
1  4  5   6
2  7  8   9
